Our Story

Our story wasn't built on grand gestures or love at first sight; it grew through countless "LITTLE THINGS". What began with a chance meeting on New Year's Eve at Hall-8 turned into friendship, late-night campus walks, endless conversations, and a companionship where we felt truly understood. Along the way, admiration became love, and love became a partnership built on respect, laughter, comfortable silence, support, and a fair share of Tom-and-Jerry fights. We are not just opposites but we complement each other a lot. Looking back, there was no single moment when everything clicked, it was a million tiny little things and that one promise,"YOU WILL NEVER WALK ALONE"..... that made us realize we were meant to be together. ❤️ #PRKaPiiiiyaaaaaaR.
